ABOUT US

PARK GUN WOOK

Park Gun Wook is a trainee from the survival reality show "Boys Planet 999." He was born on March 31, 2004, in South Korea. Park Gun Wook is known for his impressive vocals and dance skills, as well as his positive and hardworking attitude on the show. He gained a lot of attention for his performances of songs like "Goosebumps" and "I Will Always Love You."
